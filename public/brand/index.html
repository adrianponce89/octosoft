IDENTITY GUIDELINES

Do’s
Use the logo combinations provided by us.
Use Font variables provided by us.
Spin 3D logos on their X Axis.
Animate the logo on a 300+ frame rate.
Use these guidelines for flyers, websites, news or any printed media.
Don’ts
Break logo elements apart.
Apply any color not depicted on our color palette.
Use any other font regarding Octosoft’s brand which is not Montserrat or Poppins.
Spin or rotate logo on any axis other than an X axis.
Animate the logo too fast, due to legibility.
